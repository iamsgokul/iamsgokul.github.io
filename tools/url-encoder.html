URL Encoder tool helps you encode URLs and query parameters to make them safe for transmission over the internet. This tool converts special characters to their percent-encoded equivalents using UTF-8 encoding.

The tool is essential when working with URLs that contain special characters like spaces, symbols, or non-ASCII characters. It ensures that your URLs are properly formatted and won't break when transmitted or processed by web servers and browsers.

Common URL encoding examples:

  • Space becomes %20 or +
  • & becomes %26
  • ? becomes %3F
  • # becomes %23
  • = becomes %3D
  • / becomes %2F
Encode spaces as + (for form data)
