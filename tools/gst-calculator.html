A GST calculator is a tool used to calculate the Goods and Services Tax (GST) for a given product or service. GST is a tax that is levied on the sale of goods and services in India. The GST calculator takes into account the GST rate applicable to the product or service, as well as any additional taxes or fees that may be applicable.

The calculator typically requires the user to input the net price or value of the product or service, and then automatically calculates the GST amount based on the GST rate. It can also calculate the total price, including GST, of a product or service.
