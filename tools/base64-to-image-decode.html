Base64 to Image Decoder

Convert Base64 to PNG, Jpeg, SVG image online.

Home Tools Base64 to Image Decoder

This tool allows you to decode a Base64-encoded image and display it directly in your browser. Simply paste your Base64 string—either with or without the data:image/...;base64, prefix—into the input box and click "Decode and Display Image".

If the input is valid, the image will be rendered below along with a download link so you can save the image to your device. You can also use the "Clear" button to reset the input and output areas.
