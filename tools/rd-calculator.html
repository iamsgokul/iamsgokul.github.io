A recurring deposit (RD) calculator is an online tool that simplifies the process of calculating the maturity amount of an RD investment. It takes into account the monthly deposit amount, tenure, and interest rate to provide an accurate estimate of the total value of the investment at maturity. The calculator eliminates the need for manual calculations, making it easier for investors to plan their financial goals and make informed decisions about their investments.
