A Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a long-term investment scheme backed by the Government of India that offers attractive returns with tax benefits under Section 80C. The PPF calculator helps you determine the maturity amount after 15 years based on your annual investment.

PPF offers compound interest and is one of the safest investment options with a current interest rate of 7.1% per annum. The minimum investment is ₹500 and maximum is ₹1,50,000 per financial year.
