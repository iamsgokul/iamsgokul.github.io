The National Pension Scheme (NPS) is a voluntary retirement savings scheme designed to enable systematic savings during the working life of subscribers. NPS provides an opportunity to invest in a diversified portfolio of government bonds, corporate bonds, and equities based on the subscriber's risk appetite.

At retirement (age 60), you can withdraw up to 60% of the corpus as a lump sum, and the remaining 40% must be used to purchase an annuity that provides regular pension payments.
